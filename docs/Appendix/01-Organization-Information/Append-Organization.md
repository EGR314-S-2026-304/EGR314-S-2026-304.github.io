## Roles & Responsibilities

>Define the roles and responsibilities that will be filled by the team members as you work to achieve your team's mission. _Table #: Project Roles and Duties_

| **Role**          | **Duties**                                                                                                                                |
| :---------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
